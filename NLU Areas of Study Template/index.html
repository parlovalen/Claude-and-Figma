FAQ

Real Questions. Straight Answers.

Wondering what a Psychology degree at NLU looks like in practice? We've got you covered.

Innovation

How do I know that the things I am learning in the classroom will translate into the real world?

We partner with clinical practices and mental health organizations in the community to provide hands-on fieldwork and supervision from the professionals in your field of study. You won't just learn about psychology — you'll practice it.
Equity

I didn't exactly get straight "A"s in school. How will I stay on top of coursework in a knowledge-heavy field like Psychology?

At NLU, we keep our classes small enough to get one-on-one time with your instructors. Our advisory staff is in your corner for consultation and to provide tools and resources to help you succeed — no matter where you're starting from.
Access

What can I do with a degree in Psychology?

Whether you earn your professional certification or progress all the way to the doctorate level, there are numerous career paths an NLU degree can prepare you for. You can work in the field as a clinician, behavioral specialist or community advocate. If research and education are your passion, you can become an instructor or administrator.
Excellence

How can I manage the financial cost and time commitment of getting an advanced degree while working and having a life?

At NLU we build our programs around working professionals. Some courses operate on a cohort model, putting you in with the same group of students to foster community and share the load. Our Psy.D. program has an embedded Master's degree, so your time is well spent no matter how far you intend to pursue your education.
